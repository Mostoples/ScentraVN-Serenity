# 🎵 ASEAN Traditional Music Setup Guide

## Quick Setup Steps

1. **Create the music folder** (if it doesn't exist):
   ```
   SynaWatch/music/
   ```

2. **Download traditional music** for each ASEAN country from the websites below

3. **Name the files exactly as shown** in the "Required Files" section

4. **Place all MP3 files** in the `music/` folder

---

## Required Files

Download dan simpan file-file ini di folder `music/` (5 file saja):

- 📥 `indonesia-gamelan.mp3` - Musik Gamelan Indonesia
- 📥 `malaysia-traditional.mp3` - Musik tradisional Malaysia (Gamelan, Kompang, dll)
- 📥 `vietnam-dan-tranh.mp3` - Musik Dan Tranh Vietnam (kecapi)
- 📥 `thailand-ranat.mp3` - Musik Ranat Thailand atau musik tradisional
- 📥 `singapore-traditional.mp3` - Musik tradisional Singapore (fusion Chinese/Malay)

---

## 🌐 Where to Download Free Traditional Music

### 1. **Freesound.org** (Creative Commons)
   - https://freesound.org/
   - Search: "gamelan", "kulintang", "traditional thai", "vietnamese traditional", etc.
   - Look for CC0 (Public Domain) or CC-BY licenses
   - Download high-quality preview or full audio

### 2. **Archive.org** (Internet Archive)
   - https://archive.org/details/audio
   - Search: "traditional music [country name]"
   - All content is public domain or openly licensed
   - Great for historical recordings

### 3. **Free Music Archive**
   - https://freemusicarchive.org/
   - Search by country or instrument name
   - Filter by "Public Domain" or "Creative Commons"

### 4. **YouTube Audio Library**
   - https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UC.../music (requires Google account)
   - Free music for content creators
   - Search for "Asian traditional" or specific instruments

### 5. **Pixabay Music**
   - https://pixabay.com/music/
   - Free for commercial use
   - Search: "asian", "oriental", "traditional"

### 6. **ccMixter**
   - http://ccmixter.org/
   - Creative Commons music community
   - Search by tags: "asian", "traditional", "ethnic"

### 7. **Jamendo**
   - https://www.jamendo.com/
   - Search for world music and traditional categories

## 📝 How to Download & Convert

### From YouTube (if needed):
1. Find a traditional music video on YouTube
2. Use online converters like:
   - https://ytmp3.cc/
   - https://320ytmp3.com/
   - Or use: `yt-dlp -x --audio-format mp3 [URL]`
3. Download as MP3
4. Rename to match the required filename

### Audio Quality Tips:
- ✅ Prefer MP3 format (most compatible)
- ✅ Bitrate: 128kbps or higher
- ✅ Duration: 2-5 minutes (will loop automatically)
- ✅ Volume: Normalize to avoid loud/quiet files

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting

**Music not playing?**
- Check if the file exists in the `music/` folder
- Verify the filename matches exactly (case-sensitive)
- Check the browser console (F12) for errors
- Make sure the file is a valid MP3

**File too large?**
- Consider compressing the MP3 (aim for < 5MB per file)
- Use online tools like https://www.mp3smaller.com/

**Copyright concerns?**
- Only use Public Domain or Creative Commons music
- Check the license before downloading
- The websites listed above provide legal, free music

---

## 📄 License Information

When downloading music, look for these licenses:

- ✅ **CC0** (Public Domain) - Best, no attribution needed
- ✅ **CC-BY** - Free to use, just credit the artist
- ✅ **CC-BY-SA** - Free to use, credit + share-alike
- ❌ **Copyrighted** - Do NOT use
